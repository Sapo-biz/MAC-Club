About MAC Club

Discover our unique approach to creative coding and artistic expression

Our Mission

MAC is a project-focused club that revolves around the intersection of art and technology. As coding becomes more accessible, it now serves as a medium for artists to creatively express themselves. Students of any background can immerse themselves in the emerging field of interdisciplinary art and technology.

What Makes Us Unique

We don't teach coding as a technical skill, rather a creative skill. While most clubs focus on using coding to solve problems and build apps, we aim to utilize coding as a means of artistic creativity, fostering a blend between self-expression and technology.
